Built for personal libraries

Read, listen, and keep your progress in sync.

Story Reader is an Android EPUB reader focused on comfortable long-form reading, dependable text-to-speech, and privacy-friendly sync that stays in accounts you control.

What it focuses on

EPUB-first Import books from Android’s file picker or supported remote sources.
TTS that keeps up Word highlighting, chapter progress, and Android Auto playback support.
User-controlled sync Nextcloud WebDAV and Google Drive app-data backups without a developer-run backend.
Why this app exists

A reader that stays simple on purpose.

Story Reader is designed for people who want a practical Android reading app without accounts, ads, or a hosted sync service standing between them and their books.

Reading

Comfortable long-form reading

Adjustable themes, saved reading position, chapter navigation, and EPUB rendering powered by Readium.

Listening

Text-to-speech that feels native

Playback controls, live highlighting, and Android Auto integration make it easy to switch between reading and listening.

Ownership

Your storage, your credentials

Sync credentials are stored on-device. If you enable sync, Story Reader uses your own Nextcloud or Google Drive account.

No developer-hosted account system

Story Reader does not require its own online account. The app stores your library and reading data locally on your device. Optional sync features use services you explicitly configure, such as Nextcloud WebDAV or Google Drive app-specific storage.
